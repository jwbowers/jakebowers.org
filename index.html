Jake Bowers

Jake Bowers is a professor in the Departments of Political Science and Statistics at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He serves as the Methods Director for the Evidence in Governance and Politics (EGAP) network and co-founded the Causal Inference for Social Impact Lab. His research focuses on the design and analysis of randomized experiments and observational studies to understand political behavior and public policy.

He has held visiting and fellowship appointments at institutions such as the Stanford Center for the Study of Development and the White House Social and Behavioral Sciences Team. Jake completed his Ph.D. in Political Science at the University of California, Berkeley, and his undergraduate degree in Ethics, Politics and Economics at Yale University.
